🧱 LEGO ARTWORK SHEET — July 2025

📅 Date of creation: July 17, 2025

📍Location: Botanical Garden – Creative Space

🧩 Number of LEGO bricks: approximately 38,000

⏳ Building time: 127 hours total (over 2 weeks)

👷 Team: 4 passionate builders

✨ Artwork description: This LEGO brick sculpture depicts a cheerful young character holding the reins of a sturdy and friendly donkey. The duo was built entirely by hand, brick by brick, with special care given to facial expressions and textural details. The life-size donkey was created using various shades of gray and features a brightly colored saddle that adds contrast and depth. The smiling character, dressed in a traditional blue vest, symbolizes the spirit of adventure and travel.

🌿 The piece was exhibited in a lush outdoor space, allowing the vibrant colors of the LEGO bricks to shine under natural light, creating a warm and immersive atmosphere.
